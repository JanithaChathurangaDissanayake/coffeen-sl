Our Story

Born from a
Love of Ceylon
Coffee Culture

Nestled in Colombo's vibrant Cinnamon Gardens, Coffeen SL was founded with one mission: to put Sri Lanka's extraordinary coffee heritage on the world map.

From our direct-trade partnerships with hill-country growers to our obsessive dialing of each espresso shot, every element of the Coffeen experience is thoughtfully curated.
