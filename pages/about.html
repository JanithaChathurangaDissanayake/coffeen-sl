Our
Story

The Beginning

A Cup That
Changed Everything

It started in 2018, with a trip to a small coffee estate in Nuwara Eliya. Our founder, Kavinda Ranasinghe, sat under a shade tree, sipped a freshly brewed cup of highland arabica, and felt something shift.

"I realised Sri Lanka had been exporting world-class coffee for decades, but Colombo had nowhere to truly celebrate it," he recalls. Six months later, Coffeen SL opened its doors in Cinnamon Gardens.

Today, we're a team of 24 passionate people — baristas, bakers, farmers, and dreamers — united by a single obsession: the perfect cup.

"Coffee is not just a drink — it is Colombo's next great love affair."
— Kavinda Ranasinghe, Founder
What We Stand For

Our Values

01

Direct Trade

We buy directly from four partner farms in Badulla, Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, and Matale — ensuring farmers receive fair, sustainable prices while we get the finest green beans.

02

Craft Roasting

Our in-house Probat roaster is dialled with precision for every single-origin lot. Small batches, careful profiles, and cupping sessions guide every roast decision.

03

Zero Waste

Coffee grounds become garden compost. Packaging is 100% biodegradable. Our kitchen operates on a farm-to-cup philosophy with zero single-use plastics.

04

Community

Monthly cupping events, free barista masterclasses, and a community noticeboard — because great coffee is better when shared.

Our Journey

Six Years of
Growth

2018

Grand Opening

Coffeen SL opens at 42 Rosmead Place, Colombo 07. The first week sells out completely.

2019

Direct Trade Partnership

We establish our first direct-trade relationship with Wattakele Estate in Kandy.

2020

Resilience & Online Launch

During the pandemic, we launched home delivery and our online bean subscription — keeping 24 jobs intact.

2022

Garden Terrace Opens

The beloved outdoor garden space launches to instant acclaim, winning Colombo's Best Café award.

2024

Reserve Collection

We launch a limited annual Reserve Selection — six lots, roasted and packaged in collectible tins.
